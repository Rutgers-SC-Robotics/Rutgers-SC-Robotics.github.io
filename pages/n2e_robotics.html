Join the N2E Robotics Club!

N2E stands for novice to expert. The main focus of the club is providing a friendly, non-intimidating space for students to share their knowledge about robotics and promote peer-to-peer learning. N2E sessions are around one hour long and focus on hands-on learning. No previous experience is required to participate!


Sound interesting? There are a few ways to get involved. First, you can catch up on previous N2E workshops on YouTube. Join our Discord for announcements about future club meetings. If you want to lead a workshop session, or have other specific questions, mail us at n2e.robotics@gmail.com!
